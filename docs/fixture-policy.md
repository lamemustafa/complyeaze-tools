# Fixture Policy

Fixtures must be synthetic. Do not commit real taxpayer or client material.

Forbidden fixture material includes:

- PAN, GSTIN, TAN, Aadhaar, bank account, IFSC, phone number, or email tied to a
  real person or business
- real portal screenshots, notices, invoices, ledgers, AIS/Form 26AS exports, or
  GSTR downloads
- local user paths, customer names, filenames, or document hashes from a real
  workspace

Synthetic examples may use fictional names and intentionally fake identifiers
that cannot pass normal checksum or real-world validation.
